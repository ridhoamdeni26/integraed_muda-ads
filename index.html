Why Should Advertise in The Media

Why should you advertise outdoors? We Will Answer This Question

More Time Out Of Home

Millions of consumers leave their homes from morning to night to carry out their activities. showing the effectiveness of outdoor media as a means of promotion is able to compete with electronic media in the form of television and print media. Seeing outdoor promotion reaches 81 percent, one level below television 83 percent, while online media is 55 percent and print media is only 20 percent.

Reach & Impression

General Manager of BMI Research, Shandy D Fernandi stated, the increased penetration of outdoor media in Jabodetabek which reached 81 percent was supported by the high level of mobility of large urban people in the midst of daily traffic congestion, so that half of them carried out activities outside the home for more than 10 hours every day Makes Viewing Outdoor Advertising Higher.

Engagement

Advertising in Outdoor Media Can Be Information and Entertaining For Everyone When They Are Outdoors and Reach The Target Market As Desired So Users Take Action Based On The Ads They See And Are Relevant.

Brand Awarness

Can Increase Consumer Awareness for Brands or Products that are New to the Market So New Brands Can Sell More.

Effective Cost

More Effective in terms of Expenditure Because the Media Is Cheap Than Advertising on Television, Outdoor Media Is More Often Seen Because There Are Roads That Are Often Passed By The Public.

Effective Target

The choice of location for advertising can be done flexibly and can be tailored to target consumers.
